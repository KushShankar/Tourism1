Landmarks



Mcleodganj


Bhagsu Fall

This majestic Bhagsu Falls,situated near Bhagsu village,is one of the main attraction in Mcleodganj.

Bhagsunag Temple

The Bhagsunag Temple is one of the oldest ancient temples located about 3 kilometres from Mcleodganj.

Tibetan Mueseum

The Tibetan Mueseum in Mcleodganj was primarily built to preserve the history and culture of tibet.

St.John's Church

This neo-gothic architecture was built in 1852 and this church was built in dedication to John the Baptist.

Tsuglagkhang Complex

Tsuglagkhang is the place where the Dalai Lama resides. It is one of the most significant places of worship.

Trekking in Mcleodganj

A number of treks are available in and around Mcleodganj, ranging from simple to difficult terrains.
