Landmarks



Landsdowne


War Memorial

A major attraction and most visited place in landsdowne,Garhwal Rifles War Memorial was founded to make the heroics of Garhwal Regiments.

Tip n Top View Point

A small hill top viewpoint in Lansdowne,Tip in Top is best place in Lansdowne to watch complete skyline with magnificent Shivalik range.

Kaleshwar Mahadev

Kaleshwar Mahadev Temple, which is the most visited Hindu Temple in Lansdowne and is flocked by thousands of devotees during the month of Sawan.

Bhullatal Lake


A perfect picnic spot and entertaining day out in Lansdowne, Bhulla Lake is a famous place in Lansdowne.

St. Mary's Church

In 1895, St. Mary's Church was constructed by Col. A.H.B Hume of the Royal Engineers.St Mary's Church adds to the charm and tranquility of the surrounding.

Regimental Museum
A historical defence museum named after first Victoria Cross holder from Garhwal Rifles Darwan Singh Negi, Darwan Singh Museum contains rarest collection of Garhwal Rifles.
