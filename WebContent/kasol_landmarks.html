Tour and Travels

Landmarks



Kasol


Kheerganga Trek
Kheerganga's panoramic skies and vast greenery are a much-needed delight to the trekker's eyes and especially the tired legs. It is a holy place with a hot water spring, a small temple of Lord Shiva and a bathing tank.

Stargazing in Kasol

If stargazing is what you die for Parvati valley has plenty of awing sights to offer. Lying beneath the sky full of twinkling stars is an unforgettable experience.

Manikaran Gurudwara

Located hardly 15 minutes from Kasol, Manikaran Gurudwara lies deftly on the banks of Parvati River. It serves as a major pilgrim center for Hindus and Sikhs.

Camping in Kasol

Spending a night in camps under the sky full of stars is everybody's dream. This can come true on your visit to Kasol.

Sar Pass Trek

Endowed with diverse terrain and rich in nature’s beauty, Sar Pass Trek is a perfect trek destination for beginners.
