HIMACHAL PRADESH CITIES

Shimla

Shimla, also known as Simla, is the capital and the largest city of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh.

Mcleodganj
It is known as "Little Lhasa" or "Dhasa" (a short form of Dharamshala used mainly by Tibetans) because of its large population of Tibetans.

Dalhousie

Dalhousie is a hill station in Chamba district, in the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh.

Manali

Manali is a resort town nestled in the mountains of Himachal Pradesh near the northern end of the Kullu Valley.

Kasol

Kasol has good weather throughout the year and receives a fair amount of snowfall from late December to February.

Kufri
Kufri is a small hill station in Shimla district of Himachal Pradesh state in India. The name Kufri is derived from the word kufr meaning "lake" in the local language.
