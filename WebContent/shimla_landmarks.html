Tour and Travels

Landmarks



Shimla


The Mall Road

The Mall is the main shopping street of Shimla. It has many restaurants, clubs, banks, bars.

The Ridge

The Ridge is a large open space, which is situated alongside the Mall Road and hosts all the cultural activities in the city.

Christ Church
Situated on the Ridge, Christ Church is the second oldest church in Northern India.It has been a part of bollywood movies like 3 idiots.

Jakhoo Temple

Jakhoo Temple is an ancient temple in Shimla, dedicated to the Hindu deity Hanuman.

Indian Institute of Advanced Study

The Indian Institute of Advanced Study is housed in the famous Viceregal Lodge situated on top of the Observatory Hill.

Shimla State Mueseum

The sculptures,paintings,handicrafts and coins are some of the classic collections found here.
